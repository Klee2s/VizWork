The pull isn't the problem. The trap is.

Across every major and year, exam anxiety climbs as AI dependency rises — but how sharply depends almost entirely on whether a campus bans AI outright or works with it openly. The same habit is nearly twice as costly under prohibition as it is under an honest, cited policy.
